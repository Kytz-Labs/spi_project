Instructions for Obtaining Quotes and Purchase Orders
We will work with you to develop a purchase order if needed. To purchase the order go through the DIR-CPO-5651. Once that has been created, it will need to be submitted to Software Professionals Incorporated. – Pavan Dayyala, sales@spius.net
How to Obtain Quotes and Place Purchase Orders.(PO must contain DIR Contract Number: DIR-CPO-5651)
  1. DIR Customer emails IT Staffing requirements to sales@spius.net
  2. Customer must provide the following details in the email:
    - Job Role
    - Job Description and job duties
    - Point-of-contact information
    - Submittal Deadline
    - Other required materials, skills or preferences
  3. SPI Delivery Manager will prescreen and submit qualified candidates to Customer
  4. Customer reviews & intervews candidates
  5. Customer selects a candidate and notifies SPI Delivery Manage
  6. Customer emails PO to sales@spius.net
If you have any questions about the process, please feel free to contact us. We’re happy to help. Software Professionals Incorporated is a part of the DIR Cooperative Contracts Program
